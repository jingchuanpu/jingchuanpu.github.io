About

Jingchuan Pu is an Assistant Professor of Information Systems at the University of Florida, where he also received his Ph.D. in Information Systems. Prior to returning to his alma mater, Jingchuan was an Assistant Professor at the Pennsylvania State University for three years.

Jingchuan currently serves as senior editor at Production and Operations Management, and associate editor at Information & Management. He is also a member of the Editorial Review Board at Information Systems Research.
