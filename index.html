About


Jingchuan Pu is an Assistant Professor of Information Systems at the University of Florida, where he also received his Ph.D. in Information Systems. Prior to returning to his alma mater, Jingchuan was an Assistant Professor at the Pennsylvania State University for three years. Jingchuan has received teaching awards from the college/department and the Nunamaker-Chen Dissertation Award (Runner-up) from INFORMS Information Systems Society.
