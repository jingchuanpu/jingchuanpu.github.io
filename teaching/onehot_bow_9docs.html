Why sparsity is a problem
Our tiny 9-doc vocabulary has 28 words. Real corpora have 50,000–500,000 words. Each document activates at most a few hundred of them — meaning most entries in any real BoW vector are zero. Storing, computing, and comparing these huge sparse vectors is expensive and wasteful. Word2Vec collapses this to 300 dense dimensions.
Sparsity in our corpus: roughly — of all cells are zero. On a real NLP corpus this typically exceeds 99.9%.
